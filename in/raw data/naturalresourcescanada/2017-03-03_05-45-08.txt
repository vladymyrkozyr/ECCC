Canada’s 1st woman to fly in space, Dr. Roberta Bondar, worked at NRCan’s Great Lakes Forestry Research Centre. 
She was hired as a student in 1963 to work on forest tent caterpillar studies. 
Dr. Bondar visited to GLFC shortly after her return from space and was presented w/ a brooch that was a replica of a #SpruceBudworm
