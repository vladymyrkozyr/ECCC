In the spring of 1917, all 4 divisions of the Canadian Corps successfully stormed Vimy Ridge, but 10,500 Canadian lives were lost. 
Vimy Ridge and Peak are located in #WatertonLakesNP, home to this landscape. They honour the soldiers who served in #VimyRidge.
