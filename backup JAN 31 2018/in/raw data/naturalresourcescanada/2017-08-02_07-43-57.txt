“What will Canada’s energy picture look like in the year 2040?” Looking beyond their current work and expertise, participants of the Generation Energy roundtable at Dalhousie University were asked for their vision of Canada’s future energy mix. 
A diverse group — students, academics, Indigenous representatives, energy consultants, members of civil society — they had plenty of insights ready for Natural Resources Canada’s Minister, Jim Carr. 
Dal graduate Samantha Peverell provided her thoughts: “GenEnergy isn’t just about generating energy — it’s about saving energy, too.”
